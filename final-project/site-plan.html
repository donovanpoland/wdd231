Site plan

Name

Recipe Explorer

The name Recipe Explorer builds on the idea of being creative and trying something new.

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to create a space where recipes can be discovered and shared, helping people find inspiration for everyday meals and special occasions.

Scenarios

  • Busy Parent Looking for Dinner Ideas

    A parent wants to quickly find a healthy dinner recipe using ingredients they already have. They use search or category filters to find an easy 30-minute meal for the family.

  • College Student Learning to Cook

    A student browses simple and affordable recipes and saves favorites with the “Save Recipe” feature to revisit later.

  • Home Cook Sharing Recipes

    A passionate home cook submits an original recipe through the site's form, including ingredients and step-by-step instructions.

  • Meal Planner for the Week

    A user plans meals by browsing categories (breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert) and adds several dishes to their favorites list for quick access.

  • Food Enthusiast Exploring New Cuisines

    Someone explores recipes by cuisine type, opening a modal to read detailed instructions and view images before deciding what to cook next.
